quickARSC: ARSC Viewer r226.0rep

Profiling Elemental Stoichiometry of Proteomes:
The ARSC quantifies the average number of each atom (N, C, S) in amino acid side chains. These values serve as ecological proxies, reflecting how proteomes adapt to nutrient availability and metabolic constraints.

  • Visualize & Filter: Display N, C, and S-ARSC across the GTDB r226.0 representative species. Use the dropdown to switch elements.
  • Interactive Plot: Hover over points for taxonomy details; click to display information in a selectable text box for easy copying.
  • Comparative Analysis: Upload your own amino acid FASTA to see where your proteome sits relative to the reference.
  • Download: Filter data by taxonomic levels and export the results as a TSV file.

For interpretation and references, visit our GitHub Wiki.
